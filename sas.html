PG Diploma in Advanced Medical Writing

A specialized postgraduate program designed to build expertise in scientific, clinical, and regulatory writing. This course prepares learners to create accurate, research-based medical documents with industry-standard clarity. Ideal for those aiming to enter healthcare communication, pharma documentation, and clinical research writing roles.

Comprehensive Modules

Structured learning units covering all essential topics in a detailed manner. IMRaD, PubMed, & biostatistics.

Experiential approach to understanding concepts practically & Real-world practice to enhance skill mastery

Proof of successfully completing educational participation, learning outcomes, and proficiency in a structured program.

Course Modules

Introduction to Medical Writing

  • Overview of medical writing and its importance
  • Types of medical writing - Regulatory writing, Clinical research writing, Scientific publication writing, Medical education materials
  • Different categories of medical documents
  • Fundamental medical terms and human anatomy basics

Foundations of Scientific Research & Medical Writing

  • Basics of clinical research: Study designs, phases of clinical trials
  • Understanding biostatistics and data interpretation
  • Reading and summarizing scientific literature
  • Structuring medical documents: IMRaD format
  • Writing abstracts and summaries

Content research, writing techniques & referance styles

  • PubMed – Global medical research database
  • MedlinePlus – Trusted public health information
  • EMBase – Extensive biomedical literature index
  • BIOSIS – Life sciences research abstracts
  • Mendeley – Reference and research

Medical Communications and Publication Writing

  • Writing for medical journals: Manuscripts, review articles, case reports
  • Developing posters and slide presentations for conferences
  • Writing lay summaries and patient education materials
  • Manuscript submission and responding to peer reviews
  • Ethics in publication and authorship guidelines (ICMJE)

Advanced Medical Writing Skills & Career Development

  • Editing and proofreading medical documents
  • Managing multiple writing projects and timelines
  • Collaboration with medical experts, statisticians, and regulatory teams and support
  • Building a professional portfolio and writing samples
  • Career opportunities and freelancing in medical writing

Additional Componants and Programme Deliverables

  • Weekly writing assignments and peer reviews
  • Case studies and real-world document analysis
  • Guest lectures/webinars by experienced medical writers or industry experts
  • Tools and resources: Reference managers (EndNote, Mendeley), medical databases (PubMed), and writing software
